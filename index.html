KNOW MORE

This short video was produced during our very first lab session in an interactive media course "Communication Lab" We were randomly divided into several groups, and then we were asked to film, edit and present a short film in about 30 minutes. At the end, we held a small Film Festival to watch each group's work.

It was an online course due to COVID-19, and we could only collaborate remotely to accomplish this task. We spent about five minutes discussing what we wanted to shot, and we decided using the ZOOM meeting room to create the visual effect of passing objects through the screen. During the 30 minutes, we encountered many technical difficulties, including connection issues, screen-recording error, etc. Finally, one of our team members edited a 2-minute video, in which we passed passports, water glasses, masks, Airpods and other items. The central idea we want to convey is that:

Even though we are facing a unprecedentedly difficult time, we can still find some entertainment through

technology to gain optimism and happiness with friends.

Hopefully you'll feel at least a little bit happier after watching this video
